What is Pitch?

Our system is all about hearing the voices of citizens in a barangay. It is composed of 4 different services. There are public advisories, connection to other barangay, open-forum, and chatroom. Our system also gives an easy way to connect to the barangay proper.

Is it good for me?

Pitch is perfect for the opinions of the user will be widely and wisely heard. Users can give suggestions. There will also be a way where these suggestions can be a big help to improve the barangay.

Why Pitch?

Awesomeness means:
* Minimalistic, extremely easy-to-use interface
* Updates-giving an edge to latest information
* Free for commercial and non-profit use
* 4-way for communication channels
